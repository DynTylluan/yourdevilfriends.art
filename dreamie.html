That one Neocities user

Where there's a large community, there's bound to be drama. This is true for both online and offline life- large groups of people are bound to have a few bad apples and people who disagree.

In this post, we'd like to bring you back to the summer of 2022, specifically within the Neocities community, and go over the most bizarre drama we've seen on that site. It involves people who we're friends or acquainted with, ourselves for the briefest time, a bunch of kids for no fucking good reason, and a strange user going by dreamie.

Now, dreamie frequently hopped from pronoun set to pronoun set- which is not bad in itself- as a way to switch goal posts and deflect criticisms by accusing other of misgendering them. As such, we have no fucking clue which pronouns they use... so, we're sticking with «they/them» since that's usually a safe bet.

Besides that, the proof of a lot of these things has not been properly archived or happened in deleted Discord servers, so while you may take these accusations with a pinch of salt, please do keep in mind that what we say here would be said by most who used Neocities at the time. All we're saying is... where there's smoke, there's fire.

As a final introductory note, we're white. If we're incorrect about an aspect of a certain culture, feel free to let us know so we can fix that.

Who even is dreamie?

In short, dreamie (AKA dollie, corpse, bitty, chii, and whatever else they went by) is a pathological liar, and quite the toxic one at that.

Neocities hosted plenty of their sites, their most well-known ones being strawbabie.neocities.org and lullabye.neocities.org. On Tumblr, they had multiple blogs and frequently changed their URLs. They were also on Everskies and Discord, and God knows what else.

According to them they were an AMAB cherokee roma jewish intersex two-spirit trans woman and trans man (yes, at the same time... we don't know either) who has schizophrenia and other mental health issues and is part of an OSDD system. At one point they were black, but they eventually stopped claiming that (not before using a bunch of slurs that target black people though).

According to reality, we have no fucking clue.

The many lies and dramas of dreamie

Oh boy, here we go... Here's a summarized list of their lies:

  • Their ever-changing race, which was always anything but white;

  • Their skin tone, claiming to not be white-passing at all;
  • Being discriminated from homeless shelters based on their ethnic traits;

Now, is it possible that some of these things could be true in most circumstances? Yeah, sure. However, the amount of claims they made regarding everything about their minority status just did not add up.

Not only that, but even if everything was somehow true, it would not excuse the way they behaved online. They consistently used these claims as a «gotcha» in arguments, tried to start discourse or drama about these topics, vagueposted about other users, encouraged others to harm themselves in quite graphic ways, played the victim... and much, much more.

The dramas they involved themselves in online were innumerable, though we'll focus on the nonsense they got up to on Neocities.

They first got notoriety by «calling out» a genuine pedophile and consequently ruining any chance of them getting any real-life consequences for their actions- that's a whole 'nother story in which not all parties involved are comfortable sharing the details, so we'll leave it at that. How we know they lied

Their most egregious lies were about their race, so we'll mainly focus on that.

A few anonymous users (we're fairly certain they're anonymous since this information mostly comes from lolcow.farm) did some digging, and boy howdy did they find gold. Here's how it all went down:

  1. After finding one of dreamie's old Tumblr blogs, users noticed it led to a DeviantArt account. In said account, various stamps were favorited, and some of those stamps were featured in the blog they had at the time (corpsetrait).
  2. A Toyhou.se profile that used the same username as the DeviantArt account was found, and included an OC named Seth Elmas Fitzroy. The character's name and design was also featured in corpsetrait.
  3. An older Tumblr blog (delilah-dangerous) made a post with some shop.io aesthetics, and one of them was under a collection for an OC with the exact same full name, with similar aesthetics as the character in the previously mentioned accounts. That collection was made by the user @trans-gyaru on shop.io.
  4. That same username was used in a Tumblr sideblog, in which the user said they were white.
  5. The aesthetics were posted on delilah-dangerous, meaning they're the same user as trans-gyaru. In that blog, they said they were white at least two times. They also posted a selfie.

Now, you might be wondering how a jewish cherokee roma AMAB person who was previously black and is definitely not white-passing at all looks like. Well...

... Dude, we're tanner than that by simply going out. Where are their ethnic features, which totally got them kicked out of homeless shelters when they weren't in their seven room apartment? Who knows!

Their other lies (various users being predators or racist or transphobic or whatever, us telling them to kill themselves, God knows what else) were exaggerated at best and fabricated at worst. There was never any concrete proof of anything they claimed.

What now?

What happened to dreamie? Well, after some light digging on Discord we found a now-protected Twitter account that likely belongs to them. However, we won't mention their handle since not only do we not have confirmation that they are the same person, that account had a lot of triggering content and shotacon porn. So much for calling anyone that veered slightly against art censorship a predatory «proshitter», we suppose...

We can laugh at how ridiculous this whole ordeal was, though it's also pretty sad to know that lots of people were genuinely hurt by them. Users on Neocities, Tumblr, etc., they were manipulated into siding with them, and the many kids who were in contact with them are probably still very hurt by that.

A lesson was learned by many: don't just trust everyone on the Internet, regardless of how nice they are to you (there really should be mandatory classes on Internet safety at this point). As for dreamie, we'd say they would have learned not to lie about their entire fucking identity but honestly we highly doubt that happened.
