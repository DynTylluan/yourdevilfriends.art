Welcome · Bem-vindo · Bienvenue

This is yourdevilfriends.art, a personal website maintained by devils. Made with self-indulgence in mind and not much else, it's a mere hobby that I turned into my main online presence.

By all means, feel free to poke around. Maybe you'll even find one or two things of interest.

Obligatory contact information

The best way to contact me is through e-mail. I'm additionally really active on Discord, so feel free to ask for my handle there.

As for social media, I'm only active on Tumblr (main blog & art blog) and Mastodon. You may also find me at MelonLand Forum.
