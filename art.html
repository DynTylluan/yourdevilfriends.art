Commissions

General information

We currently take commissions for personal use only. Payment is done through Paypal in EUR, either half or fully upfront (we prefer half).

Clients must be over 18 years of age.

We will draw: SFW art, NSFW art, most kinks*

We won't draw: Medical settings, hate art towards any group or person

*We still have the right to refuse a commission if the subject is deemed too extreme or makes us uncomfortable.

Additionally, we might accept shortform comic commissions, but those prices vary heavily on a case by case basis.

We do not allow our art to be fed to AI, even for non-commercial purposes.

Pricing

Prices raise with character/background complexity and amount of characters. Minor fixes to the sketch or lineart are free, but fixes after the lineart will add to the price.

All pieces include shading (specify if you don't want it). WIP screenshots are sent regularly for free.

  • Digital drawing (half or fullbody): 25-50€
    Transparent or simple backgrounds, photo backgrounds must be free to use for commercial purposes. Can include up to 3 characters.
  • Full digital illustration: 60-120€
    Includes a scenic background, can include up to 6 characters.
  • Pixel art: 20-40€
    Can include background or animation, the original size must be at most 64x64 (we can resize it for free). 1 character only. NSFW isn't available for this option.

Contact

If interested in a commission, the best way to get in touch is via email or through Discord.
