Hello?

To be honest, I find «about» pages to be rather tacky. They're a lot more interesting in the personal website niche, but most people list their pronouns, sexuality, species, et cetera rather than writing something that really grabs the reader. Don't even get me started on Carrd pages like that, with their silly DNI lists and so on. Blech!

Though, I can't really blame anyone for using lists to talk about themselves. Once again being honest, I have no clue what my identity would even be described as, for a few reasons.

I do ramble on, though. My name is devils, or Bruno, I'm Portuguese, I have an uptime of around 25 years, and my pronouns he/they (singular or plural). My OS of choice is EndeavourOS, my favorite sounds are cicada songs.

I don't know much about «myself» other than that. If I had to describe myself in one word, I'd pick «divided». You figure out what that means.

Why make this site?

I don't really subscribe to the idea that having a personal website is inherently some sort of activism. I'm not inherently against social media (though I do think making a website is better), so this isn't an effort to «take back the Web»- I wouldn't be able to make a dent in the side of big social media corpos anyways.

Look, having a website is just fun for me. I can ramble on and on without a character limit about whatever I damn well please! Besides, I kind of got fixated on HTML and CSS ever since I started learning, eh...
