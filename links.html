Of interest

Art

  • Catland
    A danbooru-style gallery filled with Louis Wain artworks.
  • Furry Times downloads
    Old furry publications. If you're unfamiliar with the OG furry fandom, just know that most of these might not be SFW.
  • Chili Com Carne
    An association for artists, primarily focused on comics. Primarily in Portuguese.
  • Flipnote archive
    Archive of animations uploaded to the now-defunct Flipnote Hatena.
  • Sinkdog Archive
    Archive of Sinkdog art.
  • notes art
    Art made in iPhone notes.

Reading material

  • LerBD
    Comic book reviews. Entirely in Portuguese.
  • Toby Fox's Secret Base
    Translations of Toby Fox's monthly column on the Weekly Famitsu magazine.
  • What happened to Yesterweb?
    A case study on the end of the Yesterweb «movement».
  • Arquivo Pessoa
    An archive of Fernando Pessoa's works, primarily in Portuguese.
  • What happens next
    Webcomic about crime and Internet culture.
  • Gutterotica Manifesto
    A manifesto about grotesque art I really like.

Video

  • Catsuka
    Animation news. Primarily in French.
  • Petit Tube
    Watch Youtube videos with little to no views.
  • raocow on Youtube
    Old-school (since 2006) Youtube let's-player.
  • TWC Classics
    A lot of files related to The Weather Channel.

Audio

  • Depths of the Net
    A curated list of obscure music you can find online.
  • Halley Labs on Bandcamp, Halley Labs back catalog and the complete Emma Essex discography
    All the sites where you can get Emma Essex's (AKA the creator of Halley Labs/Lapfox Trax) work. Been listening to her stuff since 2010!
  • June Lalonde on Bandcamp
    Another musician I've been listening to for a long time, since 2012.
  • After The Tone
    Old answering machine messages.
  • Bill Wurtz's website
    The personal website of Bill Wurtz, featuring a lot of his songs.

Gaming

  • The Cutting Room Floor
    Wiki dedicated to cut/unused content from video games.
  • BlueMaxima's Flashpoint
    A project preserving Flash games and animations.
  • Moderneopets
    A recreation of old-school Neopets with a lot of custom content.
  • Virtu.Pet
    Another recreation of old-school Neopets, this one is more faithful to the original.
  • noclip
    Allows you to explore 3D video game environments.
  • Yume Nikki Online Project
    Browser-based Yume Nikki and fangames with multiplayer functionality.

Miscellaneous

  • Cicada Mania
    News and information about cicadas (best bug).
  • DeadMalls
    Information and stories about abandoned shopping centers.
  • What's The Harm?
    Real-life stories about people that were harmed by pseudo-science, religious practices and other falseties.

Resources

Web development

  • Interneting Is Hard, HTML Hobbyist and freeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design course
    Beginner-friendly tutorials.
  • W3Schools
    Web development reference point. Not very beginner-friendly and some of their examples are a bit dated.
  • WAVE Web Accessibility Evaluation Tools
    Identifies accessibility mistakes in code, but encourages human evaluation as well.
  • Clippy
    CSS clip-path generator.
  • Flexbox Froggy and Grid Garden
    Games that teach you how to use the CSS properties related to flexbox and grid.
  • Web Design Museum
    Shows off how websites used to look like from the early 90s to the mid 2000s. Good for inspiration.
  • Templaterr and Zonelets
    Templates that encourage customization.
  • Brutalist Web Design
    A Web design philosophy I like.

Image-editing

  • EZGIF
    GIF-editing tools.
  • JSPaint
    Browser-based classic MSPaint.
  • Image Search Options Firefox extension and SauceNao
    An extension and site that are great for locating the original source of images.
  • Dither Me This and Dithermark
    Browser-based dithering tools.
  • Waifu2x
    Browser-based tool to upscale images.
  • RemoveBG
    Another browser-based tool, this time to remove backgrounds from images.

Gaming

  • ProtonDB
    Crowdsourced Linux and Steam Deck compatibility reports, including tinkering instructions for many games.
  • 3DS Hacks Guide
    The best guide you can follow to hack your 3DS.
  • Myrient
    One of the greatest places to get video game ROMs.
  • Vimm's Lair
    A ROM provider with a focus on older games.
  • MyAbandonware
    Download old PC games here. A lot of them are free.
  • Rom Patcher JS
    Browser-based ROM patcher.
  • 5ETools
    D&D5E reference point.

Miscellaneous

  • Have I been Pwned
    Checks if your email is in a data breach.
  • Online Converter
    Converts media files into another format. Includes a lot of format options.
  • 12ft Ladder
    Removes paywalls from articles. Doesn't work with every website.
  • JoinMastodon
    An introduction to the decentralized social media Mastodon.
