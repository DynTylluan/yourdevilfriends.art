About yourdevilfriends.art

This website was made because we bought a domain on a whim and figured we'd give it some use.

It's hosted on GitHub (Neocities mirror), entirely hand-coded and built in the simplest fucking way possible.

Made for fun and for the love of the indie Web, it's run by two Webmasters from the same system.

About the Webmasters

As previously stated, we're both part of a system, one collectively known as devils (or Bruno, if you'd prefer a normal name). Two of us run this site, though our unique names are kind of unimportant since we front together all the time anyways.

He/him or plural they/them.

We're schizophrenic, which can impact the way we interact with others a lot. We tend to isolate ourselves and sometimes say things that come off as strange.

Despite that, we love to interact with others. Feel free to reach out!
