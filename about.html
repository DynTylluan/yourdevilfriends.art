Heya, hiya, how are ya? Name's devils (all lowercase) or Bruno if you'd prefer an actual name. I'm a 25 year-old guything from Portugal, they/them or he/him.

I'm a bit of a recluse, I barely use social media nowadays. Other than hanging out at my silly websites, I spend a considerable amount of time drawing, playing video games and feeding my Neopets. I also keep a virtual diary because I cannot remember shit for long and keeping track of each day helps.

At any given time it's safe to assume I'm listening to either Halley Labs/Lapfox Trax or Everything Everything songs.

Why?

I've been utterly fascinated by the Internet since a very young age (you can read about it here).

I'm rather dissatisfied with the current state of the Web. I won't bother writing a huge «The Web is dying!» manifesto, and while I'm not inherently against social media as a whole, the way it's implemented honestly depresses me.

Most, if not all, social media steal your data, Tiktok makes teens' attention spans akin to a toddler's, and Twitter is Twitter. I'm tired of being fed shit that upsets me daily just so I interact with it, so I moved here.

However, this place is not a statement against the modern Web (not judging if yours is, it's just not my deal). I'm not running my sites as anything other than a place to dump my works and thoughts. I just like making silly websites, man...
